Carl Sagan in an interview with Charlie Rose in 1996, said,

Science is not just a body of knowledge. It is a way of thinking.
So often the way of science is lost in the translation. Some people feel weary by just the word, "Science". The beauty of science is that it makes no opinions, does not categorize things into 'Right' and 'Wrong', but simply 'States' the degree to which a certain observation is made.
